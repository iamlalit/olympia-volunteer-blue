Publish to social media
 

LinkedIn

vrijwilligersvacaturebank.nl can post jobs as a status update or post the jobs on the LinkedIn job board. If you don't have a recruiter account on LinkedIn for posting jobs, you can automatically buy credits through vrijwilligersvacaturebank.nl and post your job on the LinkedIn job board as well.

Twitter

When a job is posted as a Tweet the job title and region are usually used. vrijwilligersvacaturebank.nl will automatically make the job title, region and link to the job fit Twitter's 140 characters limit.

Facebook

You can post jobs to Facebook using vrijwilligersvacaturebank.nl as a status update on your Fan Page or to your Facebook App.

Automatic landing pages

Post your job as a status update on Twitter, Facebook or LinkedIn and candidates will be redirected to the job description in your look and feel. vrijwilligersvacaturebank.nl will keep track of the number of clicks for you.
