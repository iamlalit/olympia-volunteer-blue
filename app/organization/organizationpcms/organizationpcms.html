About

Monarch staffing is a local grassroots-oriented company. We are deeply networked throughout the region, continually cultivating through job fairs, professional associations, local events, colleges and universities. We give back to our community - our team is frequently out in the field, helping out-of-work citizens and their marketability through career development workshops, seminars and more. We also provide our clients and networks with interviewing skills training and professional development workshops.
